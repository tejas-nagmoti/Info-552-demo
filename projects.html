Refrigerator App

The App allows the user to see what all items are present in different compartments of the refrigerator. The app is capable of reminding the user to buy something that is going to run out or reminding the person to make use of something that might turn bad. The App is also capable of creating a list for grocery shopping. There can be multiple users of the same app. This allows multiple users to take a look at the fridge because in a house there can be more than one person who uses the fridge.
